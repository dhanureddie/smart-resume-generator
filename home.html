SMART RESUME GENERATOR

A "smart resume generator" is an online tool that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to create tailored resumes for job applications by automatically optimizing content based on the specific job description, highlighting relevant skills and experiences, and adjusting formatting to best suit the position, essentially making the resume "smart" by dynamically adapting to each application.
